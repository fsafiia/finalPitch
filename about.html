About Us

Established in 1990, our chocolatiers have been perfecting their techniques for years. The creaminess, richness, and silky smooth texture are in every one of our fine chocolate pieces.

What inspired us? After the founders migrated overseas, they wanted something to give them and others joy.
Something that would inspire them and remind them of the happy times they had at home. After getting back on their feet, the founders started Faris's Fine Chocolates. Till now and years to come, Faris's Fine Chocoaltes runs to spread joy.
